Documents Required

Preparing these in advance will make the process much faster.

  • Proof of Age: Birth Certificate, 10th Grade Marksheet, or Passport. (Male: 21+ years, Female: 18+ years).
  • Proof of Residence: Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License.
  • Passport-Sized Photos: 6 copies each for the bride and groom.
  • Affidavits: Separate affidavits from both partners stating date of birth, marital status (unmarried/divorced/widowed), and nationality.
  • If Previously Married: A certified copy of the Divorce Decree or the Death Certificate of the previous spouse is required.
  • Three Witnesses: You must have three witnesses present on the day of marriage.
  • Proof of Residence: Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, or Passport for each witness.
  • Passport-Sized Photos: 2 copies for each witness.
HOW IT WORKS

Our 5-Step Court Marriage Process

We've streamlined the entire procedure to make it transparent and straightforward.

1. Consultation & Document Prep

We'll review your case, confirm eligibility, and help you gather all required documents and prepare the affidavits.

2. Notice of Intended Marriage

We will file the official notice with the Marriage Registrar in the appropriate jurisdiction.

3. The 30-Day Waiting Period

A mandatory 30-day period begins after the notice is published for any potential objections.

4. Solemnization & Registration

On the scheduled date, you, your partner, and three witnesses will sign the registry in the presence of the Marriage Officer.

5. Official Marriage Certificate

You will be issued the official, legally-binding Marriage Certificate, completing the process.
